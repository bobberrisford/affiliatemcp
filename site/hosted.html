hosted

get hosted

No install, no terminal. Sign in by email, connect your networks, and point any MCP client at the hosted transport. Solo £34/month, Pro £99/month — cancel any time from the billing portal.

1 · sign in by email

Enter your email and we send a single-use sign-in link. It expires in 15 minutes. Signing in for the first time is the same step that creates your account.

If that address is valid, a sign-in link is on its way from sign-in@agenticaffiliate.ai. Check spam the first time — the domain is new. The link works once and expires in 15 minutes.

That did not go through. Check the address and try again; if it keeps failing, the sign-in service may be briefly unreachable.

2 · connect your networks

The emailed link opens a page with your session token. Copy it, then open the connect page and paste it in. Guided forms walk you through each network's credentials; each one is connection-tested on save. Awin, CJ Affiliate, Impact, and Rakuten Advertising connect hosted today.

3 · subscribe and point your agent at it

Subscribe from the billing link on the connect page (Stripe Checkout; manage or cancel any time in the billing portal). Then give any MCP client — Claude Desktop, Claude Code, Codex — your session token and the transport URL:

https://mcp.agenticaffiliate.ai/mcp

First prompt to try: “Show my unpaid commissions on Awin from the last 30 days.”

the honest bits

  • →Four networks hosted today. Awin, CJ Affiliate, Impact, and Rakuten Advertising. Others stay local-only until their terms are checked — the free local server covers all of them.
