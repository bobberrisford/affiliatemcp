# Social video worked examples (this week's skills)

> Companion to [`social-video-playbook.md`](./social-video-playbook.md). Four
> ready-to-shoot builds, one per skill launched the week of 2026-06-15. Each
> follows the six beats and the 4:5 canvas. Scrub all data before filming:
> Acme / CashbackCo placeholders, round demo numbers, no real IDs.

## 1. chase-unpaid-commissions (publisher)

Pillar: what dashboards don't surface. Strongest hook; shoot this first.

1. Hook (0-2s): ink frame, Bricolage 800 — "Approved. Still not paid."
2. Problem (2-6s): "Commissions a network validated 90+ days ago, sitting unpaid across every network."
3. Ask (6-18s): tight crop on the chat panel, typed — "Which approved sales haven't been paid in 90 days?"
4. Result (18-30s): unpaid sales grouped per network + a drafted chase email. On-screen line: "It drafts. You send."
5. CTA (30-38s): "Free and open source. Link in the first comment."
6. End card (38-45s): mark bottom-left, "Agentic affiliate."

Caption: `Validated months ago. Still not paid?` / one line shows / one line result / `Free and open source. Link in the first comment.` / `#affiliatemarketing #affiliate #automation #opensource`

## 2. programme-performance-report (agency)

Pillar: a real question answered.

1. Hook: "Your client wants the weekly. Across every network."
2. Problem: "Six dashboards, one number due by 9am."
3. Ask: "How did Acme do this week?"
4. Result: per-publisher rollup, status split, this week vs last. One takeaway number held large.
5. CTA / 6. End card: standard.

## 3. programme-reversal-report (agency, PARTIAL capability)

Pillar: what dashboards don't surface. Keep the read-only caveat in the caption.

1. Hook: "Where's the commission leaking?"
2. Problem: "Conversions getting declined, and no clear why."
3. Ask: "Why are Acme's commissions being declined?"
4. Result: reversals grouped by reason + publisher, value at stake, trend. On-screen: "It surfaces the leak; it does not change any transaction."
5. CTA / 6. End card: standard.

## 4. publisher-performance-review (agency)

Pillar: pick your side / partner-call prep.

1. Hook: "Call with your top partner in 10 minutes."
2. Problem: "You need the numbers and the story, fast."
3. Ask: "Prep me for the call with CashbackCo on Acme."
4. Result: clicks, conversions, EPC, AOV, commission, status split, trend + talking points.
5. CTA / 6. End card: standard.
