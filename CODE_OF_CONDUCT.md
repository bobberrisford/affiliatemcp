# Code of Conduct

## Expectations

`affiliate-mcp` is a small open-source project. We expect everyone who takes
part — issue authors, PR authors, reviewers, maintainers — to be respectful
and patient. Newcomers will ask questions that the regulars find obvious; that
is fine, and answering them in good faith is part of the work.

Keep critique focused on code, docs, and contributions. A patch can be wrong,
incomplete, or out of scope; the person who wrote it is none of those things.
Disagreements happen and are welcome when they sharpen the project. Personal
attacks, harassment, and bad-faith argument are not.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness conduct that falls short of the above, contact
the project maintainer at `conduct@<placeholder>`. The maintainer will update
this address with a real inbox before the project's public launch.

Reports are handled in confidence. We will not share your identity with the
person reported without your agreement.

## Enforcement

This project adopts the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1, by
reference: <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>.
The enforcement ladder and definitions in that document apply here.

Maintainers are expected to enforce this consistently. "Consistently" means
the same standard applies to a first-time contributor and to a long-standing
collaborator; we do not make exceptions for high-status contributors.
