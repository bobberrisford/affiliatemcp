what you can ask

plain english.
not filters.

No date pickers, no saved views, no exports. Type the question the way you'd say it. Claude works out which networks to call and answers. Available now — steps one and two of the ladder.

earnings

▸"what did I earn across all networks last month?"
you getOne total, split by status — pending, approved, paid, reversed — with anything unpaid over 90 days flagged.
▸"compare my earnings month on month."
you getA period-over-period delta per network, so you see what moved and by how much.

health

▸"are all my affiliate networks healthy?"
you getA one-shot auth + capability check, one line per network: ok, or a plain reason.
▸"which programmes are pending after 90 days?"
you getEvery stale transaction surfaced, so nothing quietly ages out unpaid.

links & setup

▸"audit the affiliate links in my sitemap."
you getEvery link classified by network, with dead or declined ones flagged.
▸"help me set up Awin."
you getGuided credential setup with the dashboard menu paths quoted verbatim.

single brand

▸"how is Acme performing this quarter?"
you getA report across that brand's bound networks — top publishers, status splits, period delta.
▸"who are Acme's top publishers this month?"
you getA ranked partner list with revenue and trend, pulled live.

portfolio

▸"show me revenue across all my clients this week."
you getA brand-aggregated rollup, with a "needs attention" subsection for anything trending down.
▸"any anomalies in the affiliate data this week?"
you getA week-over-week scan: revenue drops, reversal spikes, top-10 dropouts, dead programmes.
