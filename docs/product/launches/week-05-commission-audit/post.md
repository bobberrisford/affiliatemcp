## Post

> "Audit my commissions across every network."
>

> never marked paid, reversals with no stated reason, and sales stuck pending

> slice. Nobody adds them up.

> connected network, classifies what the networks' own records show as

> pulls listed at the top rather than quietly dropped from the total.
